## ⚠️ Read this before touching anything named "ambassador"

**This codebase's `ambassador` references are CORRECT. Do not rename them.**

`AMBASSADOR_DEDUCTION = $250`, `is_ambassador_deal`, `ambassador_name`,
`total_ambassador_deductions` all refer to the **$250 deduction applied when a deal
comes in via an Ambassador referral** — that is, via a referral customer. That is
precisely the canonical meaning.

A well-intentioned Ambassador→Evangelist sweep across this repo would corrupt the
commission math. The audit that produced this handoff specifically checked, and
found this app already correct: it's titled "GLORi Evangelists Platform," and all
13 `ambassador` hits in `commission.py` plus all 7 in `main.py` are the deduction
concept.

**The actual inversion was elsewhere** — the sibling `m51-ambassadors` landing repo
was a *sales-rep recruiting page* using "Ambassador" 21× for the commissioned rep.
That was fixed 2026-07-21 and the repo renamed `m51-evangelists`.
