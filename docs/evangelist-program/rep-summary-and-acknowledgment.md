## 1. How You Are Paid (plain language)

As a GLORi Evangelist you are a paid field sales rep. Your pay is **pure commission plus residual** — there is **no base salary and no draw.**

**Commission (Year 1 of each account).**
You earn **20% of an account's monthly recurring revenue (MRR)** for that account's **first 12 months** of billing.

**Residual (Years 2–5 of each account).**
After an account's first year, you earn a **5% residual** on its MRR for **months 13–60 (years 2 through 5)** — but only in years you meet the residual gate below.

**Residual gate — 50 new accounts per year.**
The 5% residual has to be earned each year. In any year you personally produce **at least 50 new accounts** (about one per week), you qualify for residual that year. A year in which you produce **fewer than 50 new accounts earns no new residual for that year.** The gate re-sets annually — you re-qualify each year.
- Residual you already earned in a prior qualifying year is **yours to keep — it is never clawed back.**
- The 100-accounts/year figure is a **stretch target and bonus trigger**, not the residual gate.

**Milestone bonuses (they stack).**
One-time cash bonuses that add on top of commission:

| Milestone | Target | Bonus |
|---|---|---|
| Fast start | 10 sites in month 1 | **$1,000** |
| Quarter | 25 sites in 3 months | **$2,500** |
| Year | 100 sites in 12 months | **$10,000** |

Hit all three in your first year and you earn **$13,500** in stacked bonuses.

**Plan tiers (account MRR).**
Accounts come in three price points:

| Tier | Monthly MRR |
|---|---|
| Starter | $100 |
| Growth | $400 |
| Pro | $1,000 |

**Employed-only.**
Both commission and residual are paid **only while you are employed.** They stop the day you leave. There is **no clawback** of anything already paid to you.

| Period | Rate | You earn |

| Each residual year (yrs 2–5) | 5% × $400 × 12 | **$240 / year** |

Notice the symmetry: your first-year commission ($960) equals the full four-year residual tail ($960).

**Per-account lifetime by tier** (retained, employed, residual-qualified):

|---|---|---|---|

### B. A productive rep — 100 accounts (25 Starter / 50 Growth / 25 Pro)

This is an illustrative, fully-ramped cohort (all 100 accounts live a full year). A real first year ramps in over the year and lands lower. It assumes you stay employed, retain the accounts, and meet the 50/year residual gate.

- **Acquisition-year commission (20%):** 25 × $240 + 50 × $960 + 25 × $2,400 = **$114,000**
- **Each residual year (5%):** 25 × $60 + 50 × $240 + 25 × $600 = **$28,500 / year** × 4 years = **$114,000**
- **Stacked milestone bonuses:** **$13,500**
- **5-year total on this cohort:** **≈ $241,500**

> These figures are examples, not a promise of earnings. They assume account retention, continued employment, and — for residual — meeting the annual gate. Your actual results will vary. A rep producing fewer than 50 accounts in a year still earns commission but **no new residual** that year.

## 3. Key Terms

- **Billing is monthly.** Commission and residual are calculated on each account's monthly recurring revenue.
- **20% then 5%.** 20% for an account's months 1–12; 5% residual for months 13–60.
- **Residual is capped at 5 years per account.** No account pays residual beyond month 60.
- **Residual gate is annual.** Earn 5% residual only in years you produce ≥ 50 new accounts; re-qualify each year.
- **No base, no draw.** A draw feature exists in the software but is **turned off** under this plan.
- **Employed-only, no clawbacks.** Pay stops when you leave; nothing already paid is taken back.
- **Subject to management change at any time.** GLORi may change any part of this plan — rates, gates, tiers, bonuses, or structure — at any time.
- **Faith is consensual.** Prayer and vision are offered, never required, and are never a condition of any sale.
- **Not legal or tax advice.** Nothing here is legal, tax, or employment advice. This is a plan summary for review; confirm any legal or tax questions with your own counsel or advisor.
